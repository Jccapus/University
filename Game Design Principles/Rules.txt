Official Rules:

Before playing the players must pick a character from the choices of diseases available

At the start of the players turn is the pickup phase where the player has a choice of three actions
	1. Draw one strock card from the deck
	2. Choose one card from the table and replace with a card from the deck
	3. Pick up any number of cards and replace them with cards from the hand

After the pickup phase the player can then play:
	1. All strocks of the same colour onto a single company
	2. No cards and pass their turn over to the next player

After the player's turn they must pick up an event card

2. Max hand size of six

3. Last Person who was ill is the first player

4. You win by having the most points at the end of the strock deck
4.1 At the end of the game you gain 1 point if you have the most points in a company

Cards consist of:
Characters: A selectable disease that you choose at the start of the game
Vacccine: Played under a company to reduce the strock intake by 1
Cure: Played to a company to remove 1 strock from each player in said company - Player must have one strock in said company
Event: Picked up at the end of each turn to effect the player
World Event: Picked up at the end of each turn to effect all players
Mutations: Permanent change to a playable character
Strock: Played during a players turn to increase strock into a company
